About Us

iPartner Consulting is a technology solutions company, operating in the US and Canada delivering premier services globally.

We reshape how companies identify and hire resources to create a seamless hiring process.

We provide staff augmentation to remove the overhead associated with sourcing, recruiting, training, and retaining talent.

We offer tailored IT consulting, with experts available to integrate on-site or remotely with your teams.

Vision

To be the trusted partner of choice for IT and professional staffing across North America, known for our commitment to quality, proactive approach, and ability to build meaningful, long-lasting relationships that drive growth and transformation.

Mission

We connect top IT and professional talent with forward-thinking companies, delivering customized staffing solutions that drive business success. Our mission is to empower organizations to thrive and support individuals in advancing their careers to new heights.
